About me

Hello. I'm Agnes, a media professional turned Front-end Development student based in Stockholm, Sweden.

I used Geocities' Page Builder to build my first ever website in the late 90's, complete with hysterical gifs and midi pop songs. I progressed to making customized Neopets and MySpace themes before life happened, other hobbies were pursued, and it wasn't until early 2020 that I decided to once again dip my toes into the vast ocean that is web development. Even though a lot has happened since my first encounter with the coding world (I'd like to think my personal taste has evolved a little as well), I still find it equally enjoyable.

When I'm not coding, I like to spend my time drinking coffee, watching Bob Ross videos on YouTube and propagating plants.
